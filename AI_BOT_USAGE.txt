SmileCare AI Reservation – How to Use

This AI assistant helps patients book a dental appointment through a short conversation.

How to start
- Type a message that includes “book” or “appointment”.
- Example: “I want to book an appointment.”

Conversation flow (questions & answers)
1) Bot: “I'd be happy to help you book an appointment! What service are you looking for?”
   You: Provide the service name.
   Examples: “Teeth Cleaning”, “Invisalign consult”, “Smile makeover”.

2) Bot: “Thanks! What is your name?”
   You: Provide your full name.
   Example: “Maria Santos”.

3) Bot: “Great! What date would you prefer for your appointment?”
   You: Provide the date.
   Example: “March 12, 2026”.

4) Bot: “Perfect! What time works best for you?”
   You: Provide the time.
   Example: “2:30 PM”.

5) Bot (confirmation):
   “Excellent, [Name]! Your appointment is confirmed:
    Service: [Service]
    Date: [Date]
    Time: [Time]

    We'll send you a confirmation shortly. Is there anything else I can help you with?”

Other messages the bot can show
- Welcome message: “Welcome to SmileCare Dental! How can I help you today?”
- If you ask something unrelated:
  “I can help you book appointments, answer questions about our services, or provide clinic information. What would you like to know?”

Tips
- If you want to book again, type “book” or “appointment” to restart the flow.
